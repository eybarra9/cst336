What is Biometric Authentication?

It is the process of using a person's physical characteristics in order to determine if they
are who they say they are. The use of biometric technologies have been utilized as a way
for every person to have a unique identification. A number of biometric traits are used
such as the fingerprint, iris scan, facial recognition, voice recognition, etc. Many large
corporations use it for things such as:

  • Online Banking: Mastercard
  • Law Enforecement: FBI, CIA, etc.
  • Airport Security
